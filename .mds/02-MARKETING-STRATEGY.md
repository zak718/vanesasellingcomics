# Marketing Strategy

## Executive Summary

VanesaSellingComics targets sellers of comic book collections in the Northeast US (NY, NJ, PA, MD). Our marketing strategy focuses on being findable when people are ready to sell, building trust through content, and establishing referral partnerships.

---

## Target Audience

### Primary Persona: "The Inheritor"
- Age: 35-65
- Just inherited a comic collection
- Doesn't know value, feels overwhelmed
- Wants quick, easy solution
- Values trust and fairness over maximum price

### Secondary Persona: "The Downsizer"
- Age: 50-70
- Moving, retiring, or decluttering
- Has collected for decades
- Emotional attachment to collection
- Wants comics to go to "good home"

### Tertiary Persona: "The Flipper"
- Age: 25-45
- Bought storage unit or estate lot
- Knows general value
- Wants quick cash turnaround
- Values speed and convenience

### 2. Pay-Per-Click Advertising (PPC)

**Priority: MEDIUM-HIGH**

#### Google Ads Structure
See: `/marketing/google-ads-structure.md`

**Monthly Budget:** $500 (starting)
**Target CPA:** $50/lead
**Expected Leads:** 10-15/month

#### Key Campaigns
1. **High Intent** (60% budget) - "sell comics", "comic buyer"
2. **Problem/Solution** (25% budget) - "inherited comics", "cleanout comics"
3. **Valuation** (15% budget) - "comic appraisal", "comics worth"

---

### 3. Classified Ads (Free)

**Priority: MEDIUM**

#### Craigslist
- Post in: NY, NJ, PA, MD metros
- Rotate titles weekly
- Repost every 48 hours
- Template: `/marketing/craigslist-post.md`

#### Facebook Marketplace
- "Service" listing style
- Include photos of collections/cash
- Template: `/marketing/facebook-marketplace-post.md`

#### Nextdoor
- Join NY/NJ/PA/MD communities
- Post as "local service"
- Engage in community discussions

---

### 4. Partnership Marketing

**Priority: HIGH**

#### Estate Sale Companies
- Target: 20 companies in each state
- Offer: 10% referral fee
- Template: `/marketing/estate-company-outreach.md`

#### Antique Dealers
- Often receive comics as part of larger purchases
- Offer: Quick purchase, referral fee

#### Moving Companies
- Encounter comics during moves
- Leave business cards/flyers

#### Storage Facility Managers
- Units get abandoned with comics inside
- First-buyer relationship
